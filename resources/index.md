Here is a list of resources I've found useful while developing MosquitOS.


- [OSDev Wiki](http://wiki.osdev.org/Main_Page)
- [ethereality OSDev Workshop](http://ethv.net/workshops/osdev/)
- [Programming for EFI tutorial](http://www.rodsbooks.com/efi-programming/index.html)
- [Setting up remote GDB debugging for your OS](https://developer.apple.com/legacy/library/documentation/DeveloperTools/gdb/gdb/gdb_18.html)
- [UEFI and ACPI specifications](http://www.uefi.org/specifications)
- [ACPICA specifications](https://acpica.org/documentation)
- [AHCI (SATA controller) specificationn](http://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/io/serial-ata/serial-ata-ahci-spec-rev1_3.html)
